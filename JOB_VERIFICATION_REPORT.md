## Issues Found

1. **Search pollution (Job 20):** Using "Product Manager" as a broad search term pulls in pharma/healthcare PM roles that are completely irrelevant. Recommend replacing "Product Manager" with more specific titles like "AI Product Manager" or "Technical Product Manager – AI".

2. **Seniority not scored (Job 10):** "Junior Project Manager" scores positively on domain but the system has no seniority-awareness. A junior role should be penalised for a 7+ year candidate.

3. **Under-scoring keyword-sparse postings (Jobs 14, 18):** When a job posting uses non-standard language, keyword matching returns near-zero even for highly relevant roles. Job 14 (Mastercard Agentic AI) is a prime example — domain is a strong match but the score is 39.7%.

4. **Context score anchored at 90 for AI roles:** Most AI-domain jobs received a context score of exactly 90. This suggests the Groq model is consistent but may not be fully differentiating between strong and moderate domain fits within the AI space.
